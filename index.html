⚡ Quick Scenarios

S3-1: Customer on laptop with no headphones, volume too loud

Step 1: "Hey, just a heads-up — headphones needed past this volume"

Step 2: Offer headphone if spares available

Step 3: "I need to ask you to lower it or use headphones"

Step 4: Offer alternative seating

Step 5: Escalate to manager

S3-2: Customer bringing outside alcohol in

Step 1: "Hey! Just a heads-up — outside alcohol isn't permitted"

Step 2: Offer to keep behind bar till they leave

Step 3: If defensive → "It's our licence condition"

Step 4: Escalate only if aggression

S3-3: Customer filming during live set

Step 1: Approach between songs — "No flash photography is fine"

Step 2: Offer to take photo on their phone

Step 3: If professional equipment → cite artist agreement

Step 4: Document for manager

🚨 Emergency Protocol

1. ASSESS — Is anyone in immediate danger?
2. ALERT — Signal bar/staff without causing panic
3. CALL  — Emergency services if needed (115 = Medical, 114 = Fire)
4. CONTAIN — Secure the area, don't move injured persons
5. GUIDE — Direct emergency services on arrival
6. DOCUMENT — Incident report within 24 hours
